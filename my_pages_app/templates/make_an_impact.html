Who We Are Looking For

We are a volunteer led organisation and our ability to impact and achieve our target is dependent on the hard work of our volunteers who dedicate their time and effort to our cause. We have various opportunities to volunteer both physically at our outreach events and virtually.

If you would like to become a part of our community of volunteers around the world, please fill out the form below.

Counsellors

Qualified professionals who are able to offer counselling services to children dealing with a wide range of life issues including drug abuse.

Mentors

As a mentor, you will need to be able to attend our outreach events physically to meet and engage with children in our program at least once a month.

informal Educators

This will suit individuals who love to teach and have valuable skills they can share or pass on to the children. Our learning method is unconventional so it will not be classroom style teaching but rather engaging the children in variety of activities such as puzzles, reading, brain teasers, arts and crafts etc.
