MOBILE LEARNING OUTREACH

The Mobile Learning outreach is our flagship program which is aimed at nurturing and harnessing talents out of street children through non-traditional educational activities. We have designed a “mobile school” which will be placed in our core target areas where children will be engaged by volunteers in extracurricular activities with the aim of developing each child’s unique abilities and empowering them with the right attitude to seek a better life for themselves. We partner with both public and private sector organisations who provide us with access to their spaces to host our mobile learning programs.
